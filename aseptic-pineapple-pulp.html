Aseptic Pineapple Pulp

Premium Quality
(1 Review) Write a Review

We produce premium-quality aseptic pineapple pulp made from GI-tagged fresh pineapples sourced from Kerala. The process begins with selecting fully ripened pineapples, followed by careful sorting and thorough washing with fresh water. The fruits are manually de-crowned, sliced, inspected, and then crushed in a fruit miller to create fiber-rich pineapple pulp. This pulp undergoes an aseptic processing method to ensure purity and long shelf life. Finally, the pineapple puree is packed in aseptic packaging and stored in MS drums, preserving its natural flavor and quality.
